Our Sweet Story

Welcome to Scrumptious, where every day is a candy-coated adventure! We’re not just a sweet shop; we’re a magical land of sugary delights that will make your taste buds dance with joy. Our grand opening on August 31st in Sunninghill high Street is set to be the sweetest event of the year, and you won’t want to miss it!

At Scrumptious, we believe in the power of sugar to bring smiles and happiness. Our mission is simple: to create a haven for candy lovers of all ages. From mouthwatering chocolates to whimsical gummies, our shelves are stocked with treats that will make you feel like a kid in a candy store—because you are!

Join us as we embark on this delicious journey, where every bite is a celebration and every visit is a new adventure. Our values? Sweetness, joy, and a sprinkle of mischief! Get ready to unleash your inner sweet tooth and dive into a world of flavour that will leave you craving more.
